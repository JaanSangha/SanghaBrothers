about us

At sangha brothers, brotherhood is not just a theme; it's the foundation of our brand. Explore the narrative of three siblings who embarked on a joint venture to redefine menswear, weaving their shared experiences and familial bonds into the fabric of every suit.

tailoring with heart

Experience the unique touch of brotherhood in our meticulously crafted suits. From hand-selected fabrics to precision tailoring, our creations carry the essence of collaboration and mutual support, reflecting the harmony that comes from a shared vision and passion for exquisite tailoring.

our process

1. initial consultation

Every sangha brothers suit journey begins with a personalised consultation. During this meeting, we take the time to understand your unique style, needs, and the occasion for which you require the suit. Our experienced consultants will guide you through the process of hand selecting the perfect fabric and design elements to ensure that your suit is a true reflection of your personality and taste.

2. time to tailor

After the initial consultation, our master tailors begin the meticulous process of crafting your suit. Within 3-4 weeks, your suit will be fully complete with all finishing touches and ready for your final fitting.

3. final fitting

Your final fitting is a moment of anticipation and excitement. During this fitting, we ensure that every piece of your suit fits perfectly. Minor alterations may be expected. Rest assured, all alterations are made in-house by our skilled tailors.

Your final suit will be ready for collection within the following week. Our standard timeline from start to finish is 4-5 weeks, but we also offer an expedited service for those who need their suit sooner, with a turnaround time of just 3 weeks.

seamless care

At sangha brothers, our commitment to you doesn't end with the completion of your suit. We offer ongoing care and maintenance services to ensure that your suit remains in pristine condition for years to come. Whether you need minor repairs, alterations, or just some advice on how to care for your suit, our team is here to help.

measuring

To ensure a perfect fit, we request that you bring trousers and a shirt to have your measurements taken in. We take over 30 measurements, along with photos and notes, which are then used by our master tailors to craft your suit with precision and attention to detail.

fabric

We source our fabrics only from some of the most reputable companies and clothing mills in the world. brands such as VBC, Reda, Drago, and more are part of our selection. with over thousands of options available, you can choose the perfect fabric for your suit.

price

We believe in offering top-notch quality at a great value, with suits starting at just $849.98 for a two piece. shirts starting at just $89.98.
